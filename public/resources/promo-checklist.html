Pre-Planning Checklist

üéØ Define Your Campaign Goals

  • Identify the primary purpose (brand awareness, lead generation, employee appreciation, trade show, etc.)
  • Define your target audience demographics
  • Set measurable success metrics
  • Determine the desired call-to-action
  • Align with overall marketing strategy

üí∞ Budget Planning

  • Set total budget for promotional products
  • Calculate per-unit budget target
  • Include shipping and handling costs
  • Account for setup fees and art charges
  • Reserve 10-15% for unexpected costs
  • Consider warehousing if needed

üìÖ Timeline Requirements

  • Identify final in-hands date
  • Account for shipping time (domestic vs. international)
  • Build in time for sample approval
  • Allow for design revisions
  • Consider production lead times (typically 2-4 weeks)

Product Selection Checklist

üéÅ Product Considerations

  • Relevance to target audience
  • Usefulness and longevity
  • Branding space available
  • Quality perception
  • Portability (especially for trade shows)
  • Seasonal appropriateness
  • Compliance with industry regulations
üí° Pro Tip: Choose products that your recipients will use regularly. A useful item keeps your brand visible longer than a novelty that ends up in a drawer.

Artwork & Branding Checklist

üé® Artwork Preparation

  • Logo in vector format (AI, EPS, or high-res PDF)
  • PMS (Pantone) color codes documented
  • Minimum size requirements met
  • Artwork fits within imprint area
  • Simplified version for small imprints
  • Contrast works on product color

  • Using RGB colors instead of CMYK/Pantone
  • Providing low-resolution images (under 300 DPI)
  • Not accounting for bleed areas
  • Complex designs for small imprint areas

Supplier Evaluation Checklist

‚úÖ Before Ordering

  • Verify supplier credentials and reviews
  • Request and review product samples
  • Confirm production capabilities
  • Get written quote with all fees itemized
  • Clarify rush order options and costs
  • Understand return/replacement policy
  • Check references from similar orders

üìã Quote Comparison

  • Unit price at various quantities
  • Setup fees and screen charges
  • PMS color match charges
  • Shipping costs and timeline
  • Sample costs (often refundable)
  • Any minimum order requirements

Order Management Checklist

üì¶ Before Production

  • Approve digital proof
  • Request pre-production sample if needed
  • Confirm ship-to address(es)
  • Verify quantity breakdown (sizes, colors)
  • Get order confirmation in writing
  • Set up tracking notifications

üì¨ Upon Receipt

  • Inspect shipment for damage
  • Verify quantities received
  • Check print quality against approved proof
  • Document any issues with photos
  • Report problems within 48 hours
  • Save samples for future reference
